As part of a typography course at Parsons School of Design, I was asked to create a pickle brand which spanned multiple products in its line. I chose to use multiple typefaces, all evoking a “hipster” feel, as my pickle brand was called “Hip Pickles.”

The initial concept went through many edits and revisions – including bouncing ideas off of my design partners – to construct a typographic hierarchy and find the perfect logo placement (as shown in the progression below).

I created the icon, lock-up logo, label shape and layout to use for this product. Once finalized for the flagship product, the four other product labels in the brand's line were created in its likeness.
