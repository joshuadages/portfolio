ALPHABET SERIES
INSTAGRAM
Illustration
Feb 2019 | Personal Project | Ink and Paper
 
CONCEPT
As a practice in illustration and design, I posted a letter series on Instagram where I created one hand–drawn letter a day, using only black and white ink on tan–toned paper. Each drawing started with a grid, and from there I worked out a letter in a unique style, trying not to repeat styles in the process. Because there are 28 days in February, I added two punctuation marks to the 26–letter series.
