May 2018 | Parsons | Adobe Ps
 
CHALLENGE
For an assignment, we were asked to create an alternate version of a landmark using only the left half of an original image. I chose to reimagine the La Grande Arche de la Défense in Paris, France.
In keeping with the architect’s original vision of a humanitarian monument that counters the military victory arches elsewhere in Paris, I chose to create multiple half–arches, representing the change toward peace rather than war.

I finished the image with skyscraper renderings that are in development in other parts of Paris and included a copy of the WWI soldier memorial statue in Marseille, France.
