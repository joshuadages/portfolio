NYPL DIGITAL COLLECTIONS
VINTAGE BASEBALL CARDS
Photographic Composition
May 2018 | Personal Project | Adobe Ps
 
CONCEPT
Following New York Public Library’s release of its digital collection for public domain use, I created vintage trading cards using studio portraits from the late–19th century baseball team, the Philadelphia Quakers, combined with “cigarette cards” displaying animals from the London Zoo.
ANIMAL BASEBALL LEAGUE (ABL)
Four of the best players in the ABL, Sam Geraldino, Shep Barbary, Ted Haney and Daisy London are “must-haves” for any roster or card collection.
