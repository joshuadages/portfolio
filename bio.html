About Me

Hello! I'm Josh and I'm a graphic designer based in Brooklyn, New York. I am a solution-driven graphic designer with a background in SaaS, legal marketing, and years of customer service. These skills, plus my experience with full rebranding projects, digital and print designs and my personal love of illustration and architecture, give me a unique perspective when tackling new design challenges.

If you like my work, or just want to discuss art and design over coffee, you can reach me at joshuadages@gmail.com. To see my other artwork and illustrations, visit my Instagram page at @joshuadagesartwork.
